Apple Pie

This is my grandmother's apple recipe. I have never seen another one quite kije it, It will always be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well

Ingredients

  • - 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • - 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • - 1/4 cup water
  • - 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • - 1/4 cup water
  • - 1/2 cup white sugar
  • - 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • - 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F(200 degrees). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in floue to form a paste. Add water, white sugar, and brown sugar, and bring to boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.
  2. Place the bottom crust in your pan. Fill with apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.
  3. Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 35 minutes, until apples are soft
